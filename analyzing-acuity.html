Analyzing Acuity as a Tool for Value-Based Care

Overview:

Our team conducted a retrospective analysis of the MIMIC-IV dataset. We searched for quantitative values on how the subjectivity of acuity assessments can jeopardize patient outcomes finding a complex relationship between factors, showing substituting ondansetron with scopolamine patches could save $1.257M for discharged patients.

These findings underscore the complex interplay between pain, acuity, diagnoses, medications, and outcomes in the ED, crucial for optimizing patient care and resource allocation.

Technologies Used:

  • Tools: MIMIC-IV dataset, Google Colab
  • Data Analysis: Python
