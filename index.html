About Me

I am a rising junior studying Computer Science with a minor in Information Technology at the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities.

Currently, I am a member of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) as a competitor for the Team Tech division and Director of the Collegiate Mentorship committee. I am also a member of the Association for Computing Machinery for Women (AMC-W) as the Marketing Officer, managing the social media and announcements for club events. Additionally, I am the Outreach Coordinator for Code the Gap, a club that aims to provide students with a computer science education starting young, by teaching introductory Python courses at local schools.

Aside from my leadership roles, I am involved with other student organizations. I am an active member of Engineers without Borders, Girls Who Code, and Women in Mathematics. Alongside these clubs, I am also a mentor with CSE Mentor Connect which pairs mentors with freshmen mentees in the College of Science and Engineering.
